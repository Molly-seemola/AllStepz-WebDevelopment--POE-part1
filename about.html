About AllStepz

AllSteps Footwear was founded in 2021 as an online store focused on bringing a wide range of branded shoes to customers across South Africa at a lower price. AllStepz was created to make stylish shoes accessible to everyone. Starting as a small business on facebook and Instagram, the page quickly gained a lot of followers for its trustworthy, affordability, and customer service. Today, AllSteps aims to provide an accessible and stylish shopping experience for every type of shoe lover all over Africa. We are passionate about quality footwear and excellent customer service.

Mission

To provide quality, trendy, and affordable branded footwear for everyone. To give a black child a chance to wear branded shoes at an affordable price so that no child /person will feel left out

Vision

To become South Africa’s most trusted online shoe store, that offers every label shoes under one roof at an affordable price.
